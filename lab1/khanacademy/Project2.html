Creamy Pasta

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredient Quantity
Pasta 200 g
Cream 150 ml
Cheese 50 g
Salt To taste

Steps:

1. Boil pasta in salted water.

2. Warm cream in a pan, add cheese.

3. Mix pasta with sauce and serve.

Greek Salad

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredient Quantity
Tomatoes 2
Cucumber 1
Feta cheese 80 g
Olives 10

Steps:

1. Chop tomatoes and cucumber.

2. Add feta and olives.

3. Mix and enjoy.
